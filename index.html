Dr. M.A.RAHEEM

B.Tech,   M.Tech   P.hD

My name is Dr M A Raheem and I am so excited to introduce myself as Assistant Professor and Academic Consultant. It is an honour to be at the helm of this great institution and I look forward to many wonderful years of learning and growing with our amazing students, teachers, parents, and IEEE members.

Over the past a decade, I have worked as a researcher, teacher and an administrator, but I consider my most important job to be that of being the greatest champion and guide for every student who walks trustingly through the gates of learning. Research is "creative and systematic work undertaken to increase the stock of knowledge" I make it my mission to preserve their wide-eyed curiosity, teach them to fill their hearts with kindness and ensure that they are welcomed with open arms to their alma mater.

Research in simplest terms is searching for knowledge and searching for truth. In formal sense it is a systematic study of a problem attacked by a deliberately chosen strategy which starts with choosing an approach to preparing blue print (design) acting upon it in terms of designing research hypotheses, choosing methods and techniques, selecting or developing data collection tools, processing the data, interpretation and ends with presenting solution/s of the problem. For me, there is no greater pleasure than getting to know the student and watching them grow and become the incredible people they are meant to be in society.
