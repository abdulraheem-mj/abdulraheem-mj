Profile

    Dr. M A Raheem is working as Professor of Electronics and Communication Engineering Department of Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technology, Banjara Hills, Hyderabad. B.Tech (ECE) in Electronics & Communication Engg from J.N.T. University, First Division(2005) and M.Tech in VLSI System Design from J.N.T. Hyderabad First Division( 2008 ) and P.hD in ”Analog IC Design” Submitted Thesis from GITAM University( 2019 ). He has 12 years of teaching experience and his research interests are in Analog and Mixed signal VLSI Design, Machine Learning and Verilog. He has numerous conference and journal publications to his credit.

    He is responsible member of Center for Analog and Mixed Signal Design in ECE dept of MJCET. Dr. M A Raheem is a Senior member of IEEE.He recieved Excellent analytical and quantitative skills along with good oral and written communication skills.Dr M A Raheem is expert in the handling the servers of EDA softwares. He has vast experience in IC Compiler, Inovus tool from physical design as well as Design Compiler, DFT Compiler and genus from synthesis tools.
